Medicine - Informatics - Biology

How NGS brings us together


With the advent of high-throughput technologies, biological sciences have witnessed a change in the methods used to generate, analyze and draw solid conclusions from big data sets. Next Generation Sequencing is the most prominent high-throughput technology. Over the years, NGS has become a central platform fostering communication and collaborations between experts from very different fields.

The symposium "Medicine - Informatics - Biology. How NGS brings us together" will present excellent research made possible through the virtuous cross-contamination between medicine, biology and information technology. The symposium is open to PhD students, post-doctoral researchers and group leaders.

You can use this web platform to register to the symposium, submit and manage your application for a talk or a poster. We look forward to hear about your awesome research!
